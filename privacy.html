KidsWallet

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Important: KidsWallet is designed for families to manage children's allowances. We are committed to protecting your family's privacy and ensuring a safe environment for children.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Family name and structure
  • Children's names (no surnames required)
  • Profile images (optional, stored securely)
  • User role (parent or child)

1.2 Financial Data

  • Virtual allowance amounts
  • Transaction descriptions and amounts
  • Account balances
  • Transaction history

1.3 App Usage Data

  • Device information and operating system
  • App usage analytics (non-personal)
  • Error reports and performance data
  • Language and currency preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the KidsWallet service
  • Enable family members to manage and track allowances
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts
  • Send important service notifications
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Within Your Family: Family members can see shared financial data within their family group
  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for secure data storage and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our users' safety
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with notice)

4. Children's Privacy

COPPA Compliance: We are committed to protecting children's privacy and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
  • Children under 13 can only join families through parent invitation codes
  • We collect minimal information from children (name, allowance data only)
  • Parents have full control over their children's accounts and data
  • Children cannot share personal information with other families
  • Parents can delete their child's account and data at any time

5. Data Security

We implement strong security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Firebase security rules prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • No financial institution connections (virtual allowances only)
  • Multi-factor authentication support

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Transaction history is maintained for family record-keeping
  • You can delete your family's data at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Anonymous usage analytics may be retained for service improvement

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your family's data stored in the app
  • Correct or update your information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Remove children from your family group
  • Export your family's transaction history
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States through Firebase/Google Cloud services. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

9. Third-Party Services

KidsWallet uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and analytics
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics (anonymized)

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Sending an in-app notification
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, delete personal information, and opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: appxlnes@gmail.com

For children's privacy concerns, parents can contact us directly and we will respond within 48 hours.
